# Welcome

This is the new home for w3-kit updates. Day-by-day notes on what we ship, changelogs for releases, and occasional longer pieces.

If you follow along on Threads, you have seen the daily posts. They are short and casual. The same content lives here too, on URLs we own.

## Why a blog at all?

A few reasons:

- Threads posts disappear into the feed. URLs are forever.
- Search engines can find pages, not Threads posts.
- A blog is a place to write something longer when 500 characters is not enough.

## What you can expect here

- **Daily updates** as we ship features. Most are short.
- **Changelogs** when we cut a release. Pinned to a version.
- **Occasional essays** when something is worth more than a paragraph.

No newsletter signup, no popups. RSS is on the to-do list.
